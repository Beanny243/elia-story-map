## 💡 PRO TIPS

1. **Start Small:** Launch 20-30 products first, then scale
2. **Quality Over Quantity:** Better to have 100 great listings than 300 mediocre ones
3. **Seasonal Adjustments:** Update titles for holidays (Christmas, Valentine's, etc.)
4. **Bundle Strategy:** Create bundles after individual listings succeed
5. **Customer Service:** Respond to messages within 2 hours when possible
6. **Reviews Matter:** Follow up with customers for reviews
7. **Test and Optimize:** Change one thing at a time to see what works
8. **Stay Consistent:** Upload regularly (algorithm loves active shops)
